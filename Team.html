Dr. L S Jayashree M.E,Ph.D, FIE, SMIEEE

Professor of Computer Science
Convener - Institution's Innovation Cell & Coordinator- NISP
Professor in-charge - DST Centre of Excellence for Assistive Technology and Rehabilitation Engineering Solutions ( PSG CARES)
PSG College of Technology
Coimbatore, Tamilnadu, India

She has over 25 years of teaching experience and 16 years of research experience. Her areas of expertise include Computer Networks, Wireless Networks, Internet of Things, Edge Computing, Running AI and ML applications in IoT etc. and more specifically in developing IoT based smart wearable devices for connected health care applications. She has so far published more than 300 papers in indexed and peer reviewed journals and conferences of international repute. She has completed several funded research projects and currently the Principal Investigator of two DST funded projects under DST SEED and DST BDTD schemes worth over 1 .25 crores. She is doing a couple of consultancy works in IoT based systems. She has published three patents so far in the Assistive Health care domain. She is the Coordinator of the DST Center of Excellence for Assistive Technology and Rehabilitation Engineering Solutions (PSG CARES). The Principal Technical Consultant of Agna Inc. for its Medical Equipment division.


She is a recipient of few prestigious national level awards including INAE Innovative Project Potential Award and ISTE National Award for her innovative works in Engineering and Technology in the years 2004 and 2019 respectively. She won Nokia Best Paper award and has mentored a handful of Best Projects of the year.. She is the recipient of the Best Outstanding Academic of PSGCT for the year 2018- 19. She is a Fellow of Institution of Engineers and Senior Member of IEEE and a Recognized Reviewer of Science Direct , Wiley Blackwell, Inderscience etc.
