Vision

To become a Centre of Excellence of world class standard in the field of Assistive Technology and Rehabilitation Engineering to enhance the Quality of Life (QoL) of Geriatric and Specially Challenged population through Innovative Technology Interventions

Mission

  • To develop innovative and cost effective solutionsfor addressing the special needs of Elderly and Physically Challenged population by leveraging the emerging technological advancements in Information and Communication Technologies (ICT) blended with Inter disciplinary approach
  • To partner with government, industry and non-profit organizations to facilitate commercialization and mass production of the assistive technology interventions developed in the center
  • To promote entrepreneurship potential in the areas of Wearable Assistive Technology and Rehabilitation Engineering and become a Certified Testing Centre for Smart Wearable Biomedical Devices

Objectives

  • To develop economically viable Assistive Devices and Connected Health care solutions by leveraging the cutting edge technologies like Artificial Intelligence, Virtual Reality, Cloud Computing and Internet of Things
  • To conduct clinical and field validation trials with the help of health care professionals and adopt International Testing Standards to evaluate the effectiveness of the proposed solutions
  • To conduct training and awareness programmes to the concerned stake holders and publish Research Findings to disseminate state-of-art knowledge in the fields of Assistive Technology, Smart Wearable Devices and Modern Rehabilitation Engineering
  • To identify the right kind of business models to reach out the Assistive Technology Interventions to the masses with the help of government, industry and non-profit organizations

Thrust Areas

  • Assistive Devices for improving functionality, cognizance, methods of learning etc
  • Rehabilitation Devices for locomotion disabilities
  • Computer Based Cognitive Trainer for Intellectual Disabilities
  • Artificial Intelligence based Cognitive Companions for elderly and Demented Patients
  • Design and Development of Smart homes for the Dwelling of Geriatric and Disabled
  • Design and Development of interactive websites, mobile applications, chat bots for elderly/disabled
  • Technology Interventions for Adapted Sports & Yoga for specially challenged children and adults
  • Various forms of monitoring devices / non-invasive measuring devices/systems
  • Biosensors for clinical diagnosis & analysis of biosignals
  • Development of various gadgets for health and activity monitoring
  • Machine Learning based Early Detection and Medical Decision Support Systems

Projects

  • Wearable Intelligent Navigation Guidance (WING) Kit for Demented Elderly
  • Memory Aide for Cognitive Impaired Elderly
  • Development of a System for Parkinson’s Disease Grading Scale using Motor Functions
  • Development of a Wearable Arm Band for In- Home Rehabilitation of Elderly with Motor Function Disabilities
